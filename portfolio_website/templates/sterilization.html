An example of work stages in 3 stages

Instruments undergo a 3-stage sterilization process because your health is a top priority for me!

Stage 1. Cleaning

We clean the instruments from visible dirt.

Stage 2. Disinfection

1. We immerse the instruments in a disinfectant solution.

2. Wait for 30 minutes.

3. Rinse with a brush under running water.

4. Spread the instruments to dry.

5. Put the instruments in craft packages.
